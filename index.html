DETERMINATION OF TOTAL HARDNESS OF A SAMPLE OF WATER

Let's Begin!

Explore the titration process and identify the apparatus used in the experiment.

Experiment Procedure:

  1. Fill the beaker with the water sample.
  2. Use the pipette to transfer a specific volume of the water sample into the flask.
  3. Add a few drops of indicator to the flask.
  4. Fill the burette with EDTA solution and note the initial reading.
  5. Slowly add EDTA from the burette to the flask while swirling until the endpoint is reached (color change).
  6. Note the final reading on the burette.
  7. Calculate the total hardness of the water sample using the volume of EDTA used.
